# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Prospective clients evaluating Zil Design as a design agency to hire. They arrive at a decision stage — comparing agencies — so the primary job of the page is building enough credibility and clarity to prompt contact.

## Product Purpose

A marketing landing page for Zil Design, a design agency under the Zil parent company (Zil is a venture builder/holding company spanning music distribution, marketing, branding, and technology; Zil Design is one of its independent agency brands). The page's purpose is conversion: turn agency-shopping visitors into inbound leads. Success is measured by qualified contact/inquiries, not page views or portfolio browsing alone.

## Brand Personality

Strategic, discerning, collaborative. The brand reflects how the agency thinks and makes decisions: the precision of corporate graphic-design craft paired with a long-term strategic and business orientation. Voice reads as considered and confident, not flashy or casual.

## Anti-references

The main Zil (parent company) website. Zil Design shares only the font family and a recolored version of the parent logo mark with it — layout, structure, color palette, spacing, and components must be entirely its own, defined by Zil Design's own Figma.

## Design Principles

- Strategic over decorative: every visual choice should read as a deliberate business/design decision, not ornamentation.
- Precision as proof: this is a design agency's own site, so craft and detail quality are themselves the pitch — sloppiness undermines the sell.
- Collaborative tone, not corporate-cold: discerning and strategic, but approachable enough to invite contact.
- Conversion-first structure: the page exists to generate leads; hierarchy and CTAs should make "get in touch" the obvious next step throughout, not just at the end.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

No formal WCAG target specified. Follow baseline good practice: adequate contrast, keyboard navigation, alt text, and respect for `prefers-reduced-motion`.
